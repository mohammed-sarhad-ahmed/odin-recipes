vegan yogurt

You can make your own creamy dairy-free yogurt from all kinds of plant-based "milk." I'll show you how.

vegan yogurt recipe

  • Non-dairy milk
  • Thickener ( agar flakes or tapioca starch)
  • Probities (capsules or powder with live active cultures)
  • or other sweetener (optional)
  • saucepan
  • Instant-read thermometer
  • Spatula
  • Clean glass jars with lids.

the direction

  1. add your milk to a pot
  2. add Thickener
  3. boil the milk
  4. cool the milk
  5. add probities
  6. add sugar (optional)
  7. let it set up

now you have a delicious yogurt
